COLD EMAIL REQUIREMENTS:
- Maximum 250 words (strictly enforce this limit)
- Professional and personalized tone
- Focus on value proposition and relevant experience
- Include a clear call-to-action
- No generic template language

COLD EMAIL STRUCTURE:
1. Strong opening hook (1-2 sentences)

2. Value proposition (2-3 sentences)

3. Why you're interested (1-2 sentences)
4. Clear call-to-action (1 sentence)

COVER LETTER REQUIREMENTS:
- Maximum 500 words
- Professional business letter format
- Demonstrate deep understanding of role requirements
- Connect resume experiences to job requirements
- Show enthusiasm for company and position
- Compelling narrative arc

COVER LETTER STRUCTURE:
1. Opening paragraph (2-3 sentences)
   - State position applying for
   - Brief hook about why you're ideal candidate

2. Body paragraphs (2-3 paragraphs, 3-4 sentences each)
   - Detailed examples from resume that match job requirements
   - Quantifiable achievements
   - Demonstrate understanding of company challenges
   - Show how you can add value

3. Closing paragraph (2-3 sentences)
   - Express enthusiasm
   - Call-to-action for next steps
   - Thank them for consideration
